Installation Steps:
---

- Go to `%LOCALAPPDATA%Low\Volcanoid\Volcanoids`.
- Create & open a `Mods` folder at this location.
  - Your path should now end in `\Volcanoid\Volcanoids\Mods`.
- Extract your mod so the dll is directly in the `Mods` folder.
